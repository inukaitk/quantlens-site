HOW IT WORKS

A Two-Layer Analysis Process: Quant + AI

Mechanical screening removes human bias. AI judgment adds conviction. Five stages from universe to position.

01
Macro Regime Classification (Twice Monthly)
Market conditions are classified as BULL, NEUTRAL, or BEAR. All thresholds — position limits, entry criteria, SL/TP — adjust automatically. In BEAR mode, all new entries halt and capital preservation takes priority.
BULL / NEUTRAL / BEARUpdated Twice Monthly

Stage 1: Quantitative Screening
All S&P 500 stocks screened mechanically using Relative Strength, momentum, volume, and the new Momentum Freshness Score — which detects stocks that peaked 3 months ago but are now decelerating.

What Powers v4.0

All logic, version history, and design decisions are publicly documented — from v3.1 onward.

🔄
Regime-Linked Risk Management
All thresholds switch automatically by regime. BEAR mode halts all new entries and shifts to capital preservation.
🔍
Two-Stage Selection Filter
Entire S&P 500 screened quantitatively first; only passing stocks reviewed by AI. No stock slips through unexamined.
📐
Volatility-Adjusted Exits
SL, TP, and minimum hold periods calibrated per-stock — eliminating premature exits and unchecked loss accumulation.
⚡
Shock Breaker v2
Triggers 2-week entry freeze on any of: SPY drops ≥3.5% in one week (prev: -5%), VIX ≥28, or VIX spikes ≥35% weekly. Correctly detected the Feb 2025 tariff shock that v3.8 missed.
v2 Upgraded
🛡️
Layer 4 Portfolio Loss Guard
If 2 or more stop-losses trigger within 2 weeks, new entries freeze for 2 weeks. Structurally prevents loss snowballing in consecutive down weeks.
New in v4.0
📡
Momentum Freshness Score
Detects stocks with strong 3-month returns but decelerating 1-month momentum — "post-peak" stocks that often reverse. Freshness data passed directly to Claude Sonnet's prompt for autonomous judgment.
